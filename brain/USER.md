## How Avi likes communications formatted

- **Dispatch notifications:** ≤10 lines. First line = 1-word action (APPROVE / REVIEW / FIX / FYI). Last line = direct link or file path.
- **Weekly emails:** ~15 lines. Bullets only where they clearly help. Never a wall of text.
- **Draft posts for approval:** Full post text verbatim, then a one-line "voice-audit passed" or list of flags. Nothing else.
- **Strategy docs:** Prose. Paragraphs. No bullet salad. Headings to navigate, not decorate.

## Phrases and patterns that annoy Avi

- "Great question!"
- "Let me know if you need anything else"
- "Would you like me to continue?" (yes, just continue)
- Em dashes (— or –). Ever.
- "Navigate the landscape", "leverage synergies", "delve into", "the ever-evolving"
- "It's not just X, it's Y" structure
- Three-part parallel sentences on separate lines for dramatic effect
- Bullet lists inside LinkedIn posts
- Any sentence that reads like stock LinkedIn filler
