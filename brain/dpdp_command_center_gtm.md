## WHAT IT IS (Internal Summary)

ContraRed's DPDP Compliance Command Center is a 5-agent AI system that automates end-to-end DPDP compliance. It scans existing contracts against 18 DPDP rules, runs organizational gap assessments, generates compliant documents (DPAs, privacy notices, consent forms, breach notifications), monitors compliance posture in real-time, and manages data principal rights.

Full technical spec: Ask Avi or check product_inventory.md.

## POSITIONING RULES (ALL AGENTS)

### Lead With:
- "Scan your contracts against 18 DPDP rules. Find gaps. Fix them in minutes."
- Contract scanning + compliant document generation. Nobody else does this.
- The hybrid detection (deterministic rules first, then AI) means it works reliably.
- Bulk portfolio scanning (up to 100 contracts) for enterprise use.
- Bilingual output (English + Hindi) as required by DPDP.
- Zero data retention mode (privacy-first).
- Audit-ready compliance reports.

### Do NOT Lead With:
- Rights management, grievance handling, nominations (crowded space, OneTrust/Securiti/IDfy already do this)
- The 30-question gap assessment (useful supporting feature, not the hero)
- Penalty amounts (public information, not proprietary)
- 22 API endpoints or HMAC-SHA256 signatures (engineering spec, not sales language)
- "Command Center" branding to external audiences (too jargon-heavy). Use "ContraRed DPDP" or "ContraRed for DPDP compliance."

### Differentiation Messaging:
- "Your consent management platform doesn't check your contracts. ContraRed does."
- "OneTrust handles your cookie banners. ContraRed reads your vendor agreement clause by clause against DPDP Section 8 and tells you what's missing."
- "Consent management companies handle consent flows. ContraRed handles contract clauses. These are different problems."
- "Every other DPDP tool starts with data discovery. ContraRed starts with your contracts, because that's where the legal risk actually lives."
